Services

Weddings/ parties

We love marriage proposals! Every year we are excited and honored that people choose our location for this special life event. We prefer not to know until after you pop the question, (so there's no chance of spoiling the surprise) If you arrange to have friends come set up special props and take pictures, we can help them find the perfect spot. Engagement Photos Engagement photos are always welcome during our regular maze hours. We just ask that everyone pays daily admission and enters our gates by 6pm.

Photography

No Drones. Photographers We welcome photographers. We just ask that you sign in and pay daily admission. We have so many things to photograph, fom wild flowers to wild birds, Native Pollinators, and more! Some of our Photographers spend the whole day and many return several times during our short season to experience all stages of our sunflower bloom. During the bloom season, we usually schedule a few special events for Early Sunrise Photography, & Late Night Milkyway shoots. Special Events rquire RSVP and prepaid event fees. We will post those links as soon as the dates are choosen. Professional Photo Shoots We allow professional Photographers and their clients during normal buisness hours. (Everyone pays daily admission). No RSVP Required. We ask that you don't block the maze or interfer with others enjoyment. There are plenty of private spots and great locations. Field conditions can change daily. Music Videos, Movies, Commercials, Production Shoots,ect advanced arrangements neccessary. Media Media always welcome. Contact us for guided tours and more info. Media Drones require advanced RSVP and must be flown before during off hours.
